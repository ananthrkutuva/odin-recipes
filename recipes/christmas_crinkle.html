Christmas Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • Butter (Softened) - 113g or 1 stick
  • Sugar - 400g
  • Eggs - 3
  • Vanilla Extract - 6g
  • Unsweetened Chocolate (100% cacao) - 113g or 1 4oz bar
  • Flour - 275g
  • Baking Powder - 8g
  • Salt - 3g
  • Powdered Sugar - ~60g

Instructions:

  1. Combine butter and sugar with a whisk.
  2. Add in eggs and vanilla and whisk until combined.
  3. Melt chocolate completely either using a double boiler or the microwave.
  4. Add chocolate to the batter.
  5. Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt and add to the bowl.
  6. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour and up to several days.
  7. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 350F.
  8. Portion dough into 20g pieces and fully coat each ball in powdered sugar.
  9. Bake for around 10 minutes and check that the tops of the cookies are cracking as they flatten.
  10. Enjoy after they cool and harden!
