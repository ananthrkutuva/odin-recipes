Protein Smoothie

Back to Homepage

Ingredients:

  • 200mL Milk
  • 1/2 cup Frozen Berries
  • 40g Chocolate Protein Powder

Instructions:

  1. Add milk, berries, and protein powder to blender cup.
  2. Shake blender cup vigorously to disperse powder evenly.
  3. Blend for about 1 minute.
  4. Enjoy!
