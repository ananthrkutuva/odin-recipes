Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • Butter - 2 tbsp (28g)
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp (25g)
  • Eggs - 1
  • Vanilla - 2 tsp (5 ml)
  • Milk - 1 3/4 cups (400 ml)
  • Salt - 1 tsp (6g)
  • Baking Powder - 1 heaped tbsp (16g)
  • Flour - 1.5-2.5 cups (200-300g)
  • Additional butter for the pan

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter without overheating.
  2. Mix in sugar and beat in egg thoroughly
  3. Mix in the vanilla, milk, salt and baking powder.
  4. Start by mixing in a conservative amount of flour, adding more until you get the thickness you desire
  5. Make sure not to over-mix, small lumps in the batter are fine.
  6. Let the batter rest a few minutes before cooking.
  7. Heat up cooking surface to low-medium heat and add butter.
  8. As butter starts to brown, add pancakes 1/4 cup at a time.
  9. Flip pancakes when many bubbles appear on their surface.
  10. Continue cooking until desired doneness is achieved and place onto a cooling rack.
  11. Enjoy!
